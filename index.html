Research

I am curious about how people and information are and should be organized in the context of innovation. Part of my research is about the consequences of self-organization in the production of new ideas. I try to uncover the mechanisms by which endogeneous social interactions arise, and design interventions to control them in settings where it is difficult or undesirable to mandate how people or organizations interact. The other part of my work considers the reception of new ideas. I am currently trying to understand how the historical structure of scientific knowledge and limits on human cognition determine which ideas get taken up and which become forgotten.
