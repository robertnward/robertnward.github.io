Research

My research uses and develops computational methods to understand the organizational roots of innovation. On the production side, I am interested in how social interactions affect organizational structure and innovation. On the consumption side, I am interested in how the content of scientific texts determines the success of authors and ideas.

Quantifying the rise and fall of scientific fields
Chakresh Singh, Emma Barme, Robert Ward, Liubov Tupikina, Marc Santolini
PLOS ONE, 2022

Introduces method to rescale the evolution of scientific fields onto a common temporal and population scale.
